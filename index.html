ABOUT

MLRIT CIE

MLRIT CIE leads in creating Hyderabad's pioneering innovation and startup ecosystem pre-dominantly among student community through our initiatives on incubation and mentoring programs

To achieve these we conduct various pilot programs like Hackthon, Technical workshops and help students nurture in various technical sectors.

EcoSystem

AIM: To form an ecosystem where students are in authorities to take and make decisions for the welfare of the organization and students themselves.

VISION: We intend to create a surrounding for students where knowledge can feasibly flow from one person to another.

MISSION: To promote innovation and entrepreneurship among students and help them excel in it.

Facilities



Maker Space

From graphic designing to 3D printing we have every required technology to mold your imagination into reality. Here you can witness the innovations made by our young engineers in order to solve the problems of modern society. We aim to channelize the knowledge of students in such a way that success is assured.

Innovation Hub

CIE (Center of innovation and Entrepreneurship) provides a magnificent platform called "INNOVATION HUB" to each and every engineer who is desperate to prove themselves. INNOVATION HUB is known for its "Hackathon's" and "Workshops" where students learn to implement advanced technologies and also we try to create awareness about the importance of the technologies. These events escalate the competitive spirit among students.

Community Space

This is the place where the student council operates. Here we conduct meetings for organizational progress and student welfare. This is the place where students can approach for guidance and suggestions for their innovations. Our technical team will help them with everything necessary.
